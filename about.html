About

We are Services more You want !

We serve delicious home-cooked food on a subscription basis by leveraging a vast home chef network. The food we serve is prepared in home kitchens by passionate home chefs around us who share a love for food and a passion for cooking. Every plate of food served by Breakitzz is prepared using the freshest, top-quality ingredients based on authentic, regional recipes. Our food is also free

from any kind of chemical-based colourants, taste enhancers or preservatives. We have different offerings to suit different needs for our customers - Meal Plans.

Meal subscriptions from Breakitzz can be customised to suit your dietary preferences and convenience. Stories by Breakitzz are a humble attempt to bring to you the story of the exciting culinary journeys of the homemakers of India.
